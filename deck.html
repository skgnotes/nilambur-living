The Opportunity
India's senior living sector is
structurally underserved
340M
Indians aged 60+ by 2050 — up from 140M today. Kerala leads with the highest elderly proportion of any Indian state.
<1%
Of elderly Indians in organised senior living communities. The vast majority rely on informal family care — a model that is breaking down with nuclear families and NRI migration.
2.4M
Keralites in the Gulf and diaspora. NRI families increasingly need a trusted, structured solution for ageing parents who want to stay in Kerala.
  • No organised market in this geography. Premium senior living is concentrated in metros. Tier-2 Kerala — with its ageing demographic and NRI wealth — is entirely untapped.
  • Willingness to pay is proven. NRI families already pay ₹30–60K/month for informal caregivers with no quality assurance. A structured community is a premium upgrade, not a stretch.
  • Supply pipeline is near-zero. No planned comparable project in Nilambur or the wider Malappuram district over the next 5 years.
  • First-mover advantage is real and durable. Senior living communities are self-referral networks — residents bring residents. Early entry locks in the demand flywheel.

The Flywheel — Unit Turnover
When a resident exits, the unit re-lets at a 20% premium. New deposit (₹60L) covers the refund (₹40L) via bank bridge. Community nets ₹20L per turnover — with no additional capital. Each cycle compounds.

Risk & Mitigants
Downside is bounded, knowable, and time-limited
Worst-case investor loss
~₹4 Cr
Total wipeout scenario (zero sales). Gross loss ~₹6 Cr, offset by VKT land sale (2 acres, ~₹2 Cr backstop). Decision is knowable by end of Year 1.
Validation gate
2 Years
The project's viability is provable within 24 months. Year 0 spend is only ₹0.75 Cr. Phase 1 construction begins only on sales confirmation. If Phase 1 doesn't validate — wind down.
Phase 1 sales don't materialise
Validation gate: max loss ~₹6 Cr (vs ₹12 Cr committed). ~₹6 Cr returnable. VKT backstop reduces net to ~₹4 Cr.
Slower-than-expected sales
Each phase begins only after prior phase is substantially sold. No over-commitment.
Construction cost overruns
Fixed-price contracts with reputable contractors. ₹2 Cr operating reserve as contingency buffer.
Deposit prices below model
At ₹40L/unit (bear), margin is ₹10L — still positive. Project viable across full price range.
Refund liability concentration
3-year lock-in delays first refund to Year 4+. Bank bridge + turnover premium self-fund all refunds.
Operational management
Professional senior living management company to be engaged. Not an owner-operated project.
